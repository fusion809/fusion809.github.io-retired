% = the percentage of residents of this entity that live in the most populous city. 		GSTP = gross state/territory product.
GDP = gross domestic product.
/capita = per capita. So for states/territories this is GSTP per capita, while for NZ it is GDP per capita.
All figures are nominal and in millions of AUD.
NZ figure was taken from linked source, converted from USD to AUD with the exchange rate of 1.47 AUD to 1 USD. 		MDs = member districts. e.g. electing from 5 MDs means the state/territory will be split into electorates for the purposes of voting via STV. Per electorate (or district) five people will be elected.
FPV = full preferential voting, a form of instant-runoff voting (IRV) wherein voters must indicate a preference for all candidates. It is presently used to elect members to the House of Representatives as well.
OPV = optional preferential voting, where voters only need to indicate a limited, specified number of preferences in order for their vote to be counted.
STV = single transferable vote. The electoral method used to elect members to the Australian Senate as well.
M = male.
